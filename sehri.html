Sehri Time & Dua 🌙

Sehri Dua:
اللَّهُمَّ بَارِكْ لَنَا فِي صُحُورِنَا

Transliteration: Allahumma barik lana fi suhoorina wa taqabbal minna siyamana.

Translation: "O Allah, bless our Suhoor and accept our fasting."

Sehri Time: 4:30 AM
